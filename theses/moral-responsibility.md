**Status:** run 2 — full mode. Validation run for the `caster` stage. Deliberately

## The question
Is anyone ever morally responsible for what they do in the **basic-desert** sense —
responsible such that they genuinely *deserve* blame or praise for its own sake,
not merely that blaming or praising them is useful or fitting on forward-looking
grounds?
