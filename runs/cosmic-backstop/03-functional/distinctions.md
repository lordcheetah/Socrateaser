# Cosmic Backstop for Morality — Functional — distinction watchdog
> **Run:** cosmic-backstop · **Mode:** full · **Stage:** Functional pass
> **Thesis:** There is no accessible cosmic backstop for morality, because warrant requires causal/practical contact and a mind-independent normative ground is by its nature sealed off from such contact.

---

DISTINCTION: CONCERNS — 6 issues

> Run slug: `cosmic-backstop`
> Watching: equivocation on "access", "contact", "ground", "backstop", "mind-independent"; category bleed across epistemic / metaphysical / normative claims. Verdict is about whether terms and kinds hold steady, NOT whether any premise is true.

## Equivocation

### 1. "contact" — the naturalist silently swaps the term for "tracking" (load-bearing)
- **Sense in P2 (reconstruction):** *contact* = a relation in which the ground "figures in the causal order that produces or shapes our judgments" OR "shows up in what we do and how we are situated." This is a **relational/etiological** notion: the ground stands in some commerce with us.
- **Sense in the naturalist's defense:** the naturalist explicitly disowns this and re-stakes P2 on a **tracking condition** — "a relation confers warrant only if standing in that relation *explains why our judgments track the normative facts rather than miss them*" (naturalist §Case, ¶1). This is an **explanatory** notion: warrant requires that the belief–fact correlation be explicable.
  → **This is a genuine sense-shift on the crux term, and it cuts both ways.** It is *not* mere stipulation-with-consistency, because the naturalist is rewriting the premise the whole argument's validity was certified against. Two consequences: (a) The naturalist's case may survive on the *new* P2 (tracking) — but that is a different argument than the one 01-reconstruction proved valid, which ran on *contact*. (b) The realist (specialist) detonates exactly this swap from the other side: he accepts the tracking/correlation-explanation burden and then denies it is a *contact* burden — "if it insists on *contact* specifically, it has reverted to the discredited causal theory…; if it weakens to *correlation-explanation*, it has abandoned P2 as stated" (realist §Case, ¶after Field quote). **The argument does not survive disambiguation intact:** on the contact-reading P2 is the one proved valid but is the one the realist calls question-begging; on the tracking-reading P2 is more defensible but is no longer the premise C was derived from. The synthesizer must pick one sense and re-run validity, because the council is fighting on two different P2s under one name.

### 2. "access" — stipulated relation vs. the word's presupposition (NOT equivocation, but must be marked)
- **Sense in thesis/P1–P2:** *access* = "standing in a relation to a ground that confers warrant" (00-thesis, Key terms). A two-place relation: knower ⟶ ground.
- **What phenomenologist and contemplative do:** they do not use "access" in a *second sense* and trade on it; they **reject the term as malformed** — "The word 'access' itself is the tell: it names a crossing, and a crossing presupposes two pre-given sides" (phenomenologist §presupposes); "access is a relation *across a gap*" which "I deny" (contemplative §shared assumption).
  → **This is legitimate dispute, not equivocation.** They keep the thesis's sense fixed and attack *that* sense as presupposing a subject/object gap. No validity-affecting sense-shift. Flagged only so the synthesizer does not mdistake a frame-level objection for a definitional trick. The thesis itself anticipated this (00-thesis §pseudo-dispute note) and treats it as the live anti-dichotomy route, not a reframing — consistent.

### 3. "ground" — relational-functional vs. svabhāva-bearing (contemplative)
- **Sense in thesis:** *ground* = a normative ground that "confers warrant," "guarantees our morality tracks something real." Functional/justificatory.
- **Sense the contemplative argues against:** *ground* = a thing with **svabhāva**, "own-being, self-existent intrinsic nature that holds independently of conditions" (contemplative §1). He reads the thesis's "robust backstop … authority does not depend on us" as "precisely a thing posited to have svabhāva in the normative register."
  → **Borderline, lands on the clean side — but with a rider.** The mapping robust-backstop ⟶ svabhāva-bearing-ground is defensible: 00-thesis's robust sense ("authority does not depend on us, our practices, or our constitution") and P3 ("mind-independent and transcendent") do assert exactly the independence-from-conditions that *svabhāva* names. So the contemplative is not equivocating on "ground"; he is translating the thesis's term into his vocabulary faithfully. **The rider:** he then generalizes "lacks svabhāva" from the backstop to *everything* (tables, fire, nirvāṇa). That generalization is sound *within* Madhyamaka but is doing the real work of his verdict — see Category bleed #6, where the move from "no ultimate ground" to "morality functions fine" crosses a kind-boundary.

### Clean on this axis
- **"backstop"** — pinned to the robust sense by 00-thesis and held there by every voice. No one smuggles the deflationary sense ("whatever stabilizes practice from within") back in as if it answered the robust thesis. The naturalist explicitly keeps them apart ("the deflationary 'backstop' the thesis explicitly sets aside," naturalist §Case) and the contemplative's "conventional" footing is openly offered as the deflationary thing, not passed off as the robust one. **Stable.**
- **"mind-independent"** — held steady as "authority does not depend on us, our practices, or our constitution" (P3) across naturalist, realist, phenomenologist. The rationalist's "mind-*involving* without being mind-*dependent*" (rationalist §constitutivist move) is a **stipulated third category, announced as such**, not a covert shift — legitimate (see Category bleed #5 for the load it carries). **Stable as a term; watch the bridge.**
- **"a priori insight"** — naturalist ("a *name* for the reliability we have not yet explained, not an explanation") and realist ("the relation by which we know mathematics, logic, and the self-evident normative truths") use it in the *same* sense and disagree about whether it confers warrant. That is a real disagreement, not equivocation. **Stable.**

## Category bleed

### 4. The realist's slide: necessity (metaphysical/conceptual) → reliability (epistemic) (the flagged move)
- **Crossing:** The realist's safety/modal reply argues "where truths are necessary, the 'we might easily have landed on a falsehood' worry has no purchase, and reliability is explicable without any causal link" (realist §Case, Clarke-Doane point; restated §What I deny, 4th bullet).
  → A claim of kind **metaphysical/conceptual** (normative truths are *necessary* — true in all nearby worlds) is deployed to discharge a burden of kind **epistemic** (our *faculty* is reliable; the belief–fact correlation is explained). The naturalist names the gap precisely: "necessity of the *truths* does not explain the reliability of the *faculty* … a broken clock's reading is necessitated too" (naturalist §What I deny). The standard the realist's claim *was* given (modal/metaphysical: no near world where the truth differs) is not the standard the reliability challenge *needs* (explanatory: why does *this faculty* covary with *those facts*). **Whether the bridge holds is a substantive dispute I do not adjudicate** — but the synthesizer should see that the realist's "reliability is explicable" rests on a metaphysical premise carrying an epistemic conclusion, and the bridge between them is asserted via the safety-condition, not derived. Flag the joint, not the verdict.

### 5. The rationalist's "constitutive" relation: normative authority → epistemic access, via one term
- **Crossing:** Constitutivism is summoned to answer *two distinct demands at once* under the single relation "constitutive": (a) the **normative/metaphysical** demand — what makes the moral law *authoritative/binding* (answer: it is the form of rational volition, self-legislated, inescapable); (b) the **epistemic** demand of P1/P2 — what gives us *warrant/access* to it. The rationalist asserts the constitutive relation satisfies *both*: "genuine access, not posited and inert … the constitutivist owes *no* tracking story, because there is nothing external to track" (rationalist §why genuine access).
  → The inference "the norm is constitutive of my agency (a claim about the *source of its authority*) ∴ I have warrant to believe its deliverances (a claim about *access*)" moves from a normative-constitution claim to an epistemic-access claim. That may be the correct anti-gap move — but it is a **crossing from the normative register to the epistemic register**, and the bridge is the contested identity "being-under-a-constitutive-standard = being-in-a-warrant-relation." 01-reconstruction explicitly left open whether a "*third* warrant-conferring relation" exists; the rationalist supplies one, but does so by *collapsing* the authority-question and the access-question into one relation. The synthesizer should mark that the rationalist's defeat of P2 depends on that collapse being legitimate, not on tracking being supplied.
- **Sub-note (clean):** the rationalist's "mind-involving without mind-dependent" is announced as a stipulated third box, so it is not equivocation (see Clean list) — but it is the *device* that lets authority and access fuse without falling into either P3-transcendence or P4-entanglement. The whole anti-P4 case rides on that one stipulated category doing real metaphysical work. Not a defect to rule on; a joint to watch.

### 6. The contemplative's two-truths move: metaphysical absence → normative sufficiency (the flagged move)
- **Crossing:** The argument runs: *ultimately* there is no svabhāva-bearing ground (a **metaphysical** claim — emptiness of own-being) ∴ *conventionally* morality "functions perfectly well," "is sturdy enough to live and die by," "cruelty is worse than kindness" (a **normative** claim). The bridge is "emptiness is *what makes it work*" and "you cannot be deprived of what could never have existed" (contemplative §2, §shared-assumption).
  → This is the cleanest instance of the bleed the role exists to catch. A **metaphysical** result (nothing has independent own-being) is converted into a **normative** reassurance (the conventional verdicts retain their grip). The two-truths doctrine is *designed* to hold these without competition — that is its explicit content (MMK 24.8–10, quoted) — so within Madhyamaka the move is principled, not sloppy. **But the bridge is supplied by a framework premise** ("the conventional is not demoted by being empty"), and that premise is exactly what an interlocutor who feels the thesis's anxiety is denying. The contemplative grants the thesis's *metaphysical/epistemic* conclusion (no accessible robust ground — he affirms P4 outright) and then **re-classifies it as good news on normative grounds**. That re-classification is a category crossing: the thesis's conclusion was offered as epistemic/metaphysical (no *access* to a *ground*), and the contemplative answers it as normative (morality *needs* no such thing). He may be right that the thesis's "anxiety" was a category error all along — but the synthesizer must see that he does not refute any premise (he says so: "the error is not in any premise"); he relocates the dispute from the epistemic register to the normative-therapeutic one. That is a legitimate philosophical move *only if* the two-truths bridge holds; as a *distinction* matter, flag that the contemplative's verdict ("conclusion correct, framing confused") is doing its work by **switching the kind of claim under discussion**, not by meeting the thesis on its own (epistemic) ground.

### Sub-flag: naturalist's "near-analytic" P1 and "conceptual" P4
- The naturalist accepts P1 as "near-analytic" and P4 "on the conceptual reading." 01-reconstruction's variant 2 indeed reframes P4 as **conceptual** (about the *function* of the word "backstop") rather than **metaphysical** (about what is contingently natural). The naturalist adopts the conceptual reading cleanly and consistently — **no bleed here**, noted because the realist attacks P4/P2 as *covertly metaphysical-naturalist* ("simply restates naturalism," realist VERDICT). So the council disagrees about *which kind* P4 is: conceptual (naturalist, per variant 2) vs. smuggled-metaphysical-naturalism (realist). That is a live, legitimate dispute about the category of P4 — the synthesizer should treat "is P4 conceptual or metaphysical?" as an open joint, since the thesis's defense against question-begging (variant 2) *depends* on it being conceptual.

## One-line guidance for the synthesizer
The single most important joint: **P2 is being argued on two non-identical senses — "contact" (the validated premise) and "tracking/correlation-explanation" (the defensible premise) — and the naturalist↔realist clash is partly an artifact of that swap.** Pin which P2 is on trial before scoring the crux. Secondary: the rationalist (#5) and contemplative (#6) both win only by crossing register — authority→access, and metaphysical-absence→normative-sufficiency respectively — so their "P2 is false" / "framing is confused" verdicts are not refutations *on the thesis's own epistemic terms* and should not be tallied as if they were.
